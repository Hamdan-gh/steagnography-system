║ THE PROBLEM (Quick Summary)                                             ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

Error Message:
  "No 'Access-Control-Allow-Origin' header is present"

Root Cause:
  Your Render free tier API goes to sleep after 15 minutes of inactivity.
  First request after sleep times out (30-60s wake-up time).
  Browser shows this timeout as a CORS error.

Quick Fix:
  Open https://stegagen-api.onrender.com/api/health in browser
  Wait 60 seconds
  Try your frontend again
  Done! ✓
